WHAT IS NEW?

The City of Bellevue is working on the next phase of expanding Meydenbauer Bay Park, with a focus on creating a stronger connection to downtown. This project extends south of Lake Washington Blvd NE and up to 100th Ave NE. We are in the early design phase and hope to get your input along the way!

Visit The City of Bellevue’s Meydenbauer Bay Park and Grand Connection web pages to learn more about what currently exists and how this project fits into a broader city plan.

We encourage you to sign up to our email list to stay up to date on design progress.
